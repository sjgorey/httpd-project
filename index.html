Guy Langevin for Governor of Maine 2026

About Guy Langevin

Guy Langevin, a dedicated family man, recently celebrated his 60th birthday. He has a deep love for Maine and its people, and he is passionate about making a difference in the community.

Interests

Guy enjoys skiing and fishing in Maine's beautiful outdoors, but his true passion lies in golf. He believes in the importance of recreation and the role it plays in bringing people together.

Professional Experience

Guy recently retired from Dead River Company, where he served as the Head of Human Resources. His leadership and dedication have also extended to serving on numerous charity boards across Maine, demonstrating his commitment to giving back to the community.
